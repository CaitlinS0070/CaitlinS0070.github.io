01
JavaScript · Mapbox

Pennsylvania Underground Mines

An interactive map of underground mine locations across Pennsylvania. Users can click on any mine marker to view the site name in a popup, and zoom in to explore individual locations. A legend is included to help users understand the symbology. The map automatically opens to the bounds of Pennsylvania.

02
ArcGIS Online · Zone Lookup App

Fire Stations in Massachusetts

An interactive ArcGIS Online Zone Lookup app mapping all 771 fire department locations across Massachusetts, with county-level population data. Users can search by address or current location to find nearby fire stations, click within the map to explore a specific area, or click directly on a fire station symbol to view its address and details. The map was configured using MassGIS data and includes custom symbology, improved pop-up windows, and a description of the data's public safety significance. Population data by county is included to provide context for service coverage.

03
Survey123 · Web Surveying

Fire Hazard Self-Reporting Survey

A Survey123 form enabling residents of West Springfield, Massachusetts to self-report fire hazards in their homes and request assistance. Responses include a location-based question that generates spatial data, allowing fire departments or community organizations to prioritize outreach and home safety visits based on where hazards are concentrated. This Survey123 was created for the final portfolio as an improved survey to the initial one submitted. I took into consideration the comments on description and effective questions.
